Carne Guisada Recipe

This recipe will teach you the proper components necessary to make the perfect Carne Guisada, you'll make your abuela proud with this. Listed below will be each of the ingredients followed by the steps on how to construct this dish . If followed correctly, you will have the best Carne Guisade north of the border.

  • ¼ cup olive oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 3 pounds stew meat (cut into small bite size pieces, 1-inch cubes)
  • 1 large onion (or 2 small ones, chopped)
  • 1 medium green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3 large roma tomatoes (diced)
  • 3 large roma tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons cumin (ground)
  • 1½ teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cube beef bouillon
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour (or more for thicker gravy)
  1. In a large pot, bring 4-6 quarts of water and 1-1 1/2 teaspoons of salt to a boil
  2. Add 12 ounces of spaghetti to the poy, stir to separate noodles and prevent sticking then bring water back to a boil
  3. Cook spaghetti uncovered according to package directions; start checking for doneness (How cooked it is) 2 minutes before the suggested finished time.
  4. Drain spaghetti, keep up to 1 cup of cooking water to add to sauce.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce with a splash of the cooking water and stir , add more cooking water until you have desired thickness. Optional : Add spaghetti sauce and stir in with noodles
